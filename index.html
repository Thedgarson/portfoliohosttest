About me


Hello! My name is Theodore Edgar Sondakh, and I’m a creative individual with a
strong passion for photography. I love capturing random things and turning everyday
scenes into interesting visuals. I’m currently still in college, working on building my
skills and gaining knowledge in various technical fields.


I have a solid understanding of programming languages and tools such as CSS, C#, Java, HTML, and MySQL, along with design skills in Figma. I enjoy taking ideas and bringing them to life through both design and code, making functional yet visually appealing projects. Apart from that, I’m also passionate about designing things, playing the guitar, and listening to music.


When I’m not working on projects or learning something new, I like to spend time alone, either playing games, walking by myself, or just enjoying some quiet time. My goal for the future is to combine my hobbies and skills to create unique projects that merge my love for photography, design, and coding. I want to build experiences that are not just functional but also aesthetically pleasing, whether it’s through creative websites, software solutions, or visual designs that tell a story.
